WHAT IS MONTANA STATE PARKS PAGE ABOUT?

A government agency that targets friends and family who are intrested in exploring the beauty of montana outdoors. The goals are to provide an extraordinary experiece for the visitors and to keep the state park system strong now and far genrations to come

THE PROBLEM:

As a team, we feel the website has a bad user interface and the navigation is really confusing.

THE SOLUTION:

To redesign and restructure both the desktop + mobile interfaces to make it easier for users to navigate, and ultimately increase visibility to locate a park or an activity.
